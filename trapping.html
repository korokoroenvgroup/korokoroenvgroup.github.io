TRAPPING

We are working towards a predator-free Lower Hutt, providing rat traps and advice to any residents who want to start backyard trapping. In the last three years the number of backyard traps and kills have significantly increased, many of them logged on the trapNZ website.

Recently Andre Oliver and other volunteers have started setting traplines on reserve lands with some impressive results.

If you would like a Victor rat trap and box for your backyard, please contact Daniel.
